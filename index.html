What's inside

A tracker that grew a coach.

01

Your plan, not a template

Answer a few questions — or bring your own split — and get a week matched to your days, equipment and level. Skip, crush, or tweak a session and next week adapts. A rules-based engine, never a black box.

02

Trains around injuries

Tell Nemku a nagging shoulder and it avoids the movements that aggravate it while preserving the muscle volume — through safe alternatives, not by dropping the work.

03

Knows your gym

Set what each location has and Nemku only prescribes lifts you can actually do — swapping an unavailable exercise for an equivalent and capping overload to the weight you can load.

04

Quiet company

A capybara named Capi lives in the app. He doesn't cheer, doesn't nag, and doesn't send notifications at 6am. He mostly just believes in you, quietly.

Questions

When does Nemku launch?+

Soon, on iOS. Leave your email above and you'll get one message the day it's on the App Store — nothing before, nothing after.

Do I need an account?+

No. There's no sign-up and no login. The app is fully usable the moment it launches, and sync rides your own private iCloud with no credentials to manage.

Is it free?+

Manual logging, quick-log, charts and iCloud sync are free forever. Nemku Pro unlocks the adaptive plan engine and muscle-balance analysis. Pricing is announced at launch.

What happens to my data?+

It stays yours. Your log lives on your device and in your private iCloud. There's no server the developer can read, no analytics, and no third parties. Health data never leaves the device.
