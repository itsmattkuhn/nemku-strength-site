Nemku Strength Features Privacy Support
Privacy policy

Your data stays yours.

Effective July 10, 2026

Nemku Strength is built by one person, funded by nothing, and has no interest in your data. The short version: your training data stays on your device and in your own iCloud. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no third-party services. I cannot see your data — not your workouts, not your weight, not anything.

What the app stores

Your training log. Workouts, sets, weights, distances, plan settings, custom exercises, and gyms are stored on your device.

iCloud sync (optional). If you leave iCloud sync on, your training log is also stored in your private iCloud database so it can back up and follow you to a new phone. This database belongs to your Apple ID. Apple hosts it; I have no access to it, no server of my own, and no way to read it. Turning sync off (or erasing your data in the app) deletes the iCloud copy.

Health-derived data never goes to iCloud. Metrics the app reads from Apple Health — heart rate, calories, elevation on imported watch workouts, and your body weight — stay on the device that read them. Each of your devices re-reads those numbers from its own Apple Health store instead of syncing them.

Apple Health

With your permission, the app:

  • RReads your workouts, heart rate, and active energy — so workouts recorded on Apple Watch (or other apps) appear in your training log with their metrics — and your body weight, to score bodyweight movements and improve calorie estimates.
  • WWrites your finished workouts and estimated calories to Apple Health, so they appear in Apple Fitness and count toward your rings.

Health data is used only for the features above. It is never sold, shared, transmitted to any server, or used for anything else. You can change or revoke Health access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

What the app does not do

  • No accounts or sign-up — there is nothing to register.
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind.
  • No advertising, ad identifiers, or tracking.
  • No third-party SDKs that phone home.
  • No data collection by the developer. None.

Deleting your data

Settings → Erase all data in the app removes your training log from the device and deletes the iCloud copy. Workouts the app saved to Apple Health are managed in the Health app and can be deleted there.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Given what the app is, don't expect much movement.
